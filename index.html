Welcome To Bradshaw Christian School

At Bradshaw Christian School, our purpose is simple and deeply rooted. We exist to help students discover who they are in Christ and to guide them toward lives filled with wisdom, character, and purpose. Every classroom, program, and relationship is shaped by the belief that education is more than academics. It is the formation of the whole child, nurturing their spiritual, intellectual, social, and emotional growth.


We walk alongside students as they learn to see the world through a biblical lens, understand their God-given gifts, and respond to life with courage, compassion, and integrity. Our mission is not only to prepare students for college and career, but to prepare them for a meaningful life — one grounded in truth, strengthened by faith, and lived for the good of others.
